Summary

I am a Robotics Engineer with expertise in autonomous systems, machine learning, and embedded platforms. My work focuses on developing intelligent robotic systems that combine perception, planning, and control to achieve reliable real-world operation. I recently completed a Master of Engineering in Robotics at the University of Maryland, where I concentrated on autonomy algorithms, system integration, and robotic control.

My technical skill set includes Python, C++, ROS2, TensorFlow, PyTorch, and OpenCV, along with experience using Webots, Gazebo, SolidWorks, MATLAB, and embedded hardware such as Raspberry Pi. I specialize in real-time control, reinforcement learning, sensor fusion (IMU, LiDAR, RGB-D), and computer vision pipelines.

I have worked on autonomous mobile robots, robotic arm manipulation and motion planning, real-time perception systems, and algorithmic development for navigation and task execution. I enjoy working at the intersection of software, control, and intelligent decision-making—focusing on building robust and adaptive robotic behaviors.
